About

Welcome to Liquid Stats. This project uses the Untappd API, and allows you to keep track of whats beers you have had, your top rated beers, badges you earned, and statics about your Untappd checkins.


Recent Checkins


The recent checkins page will display your 25 most recent checkins.


Top Beers


The top beers page is what it sounds. It will display the top beers you have checked in, based on your rating of those beers. The number of beers displayed will be based on a ratings threshold (i.e all ratings > x)


Stats


This page will display diferent stats including bar graphs, pie charts, a map, and a table. Bar graphs will show for your ratings, community ratings, ABV, and IBU. Pie charts will display nmber of beers by year, month, and day of the week. A map will display the location of the beers. a table will display each beer and has columns for brewey info, beer info with description, style info, your rating, community ratinng, ABV, and IBU.
