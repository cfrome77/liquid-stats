About Liquid Stats

Track. Analyze. Discover.

Liquid Stats connects to the Untappd API to help you explore your beer history, top-rated brews, earned badges, and deep analytics about your check-ins.

history Recent Checkins

Displays your 25 most recent check-ins with full beer and brewery details.

emoji_events Top Beers

Shows your highest-rated beers based on a customizable rating threshold.

bar_chart Stats

Interactive charts, brewery maps, and tables for ratings, ABV, IBU, and beer distribution over time.

favorite Wishlist

Displays beers saved to your Untappd wishlist.
