About

Welcome to Liquid Stats. This project uses the Untappd API, and allows you to keep track of whats beers you have had, your top rated beers, badges you earned, and statics about your Untappd checkins.

Recent Checkins

The recent checkins page will display your 25 most recent checkins.

Top Beers

The top beers page is what it sounds. It will display the top beers you have checked in, based on your rating of those beers. The number of beers displayed will be based on a ratings threshold (i.e all ratings > x)

Stats

This page will display different stats, using bar graphs, pie charts, a map, and a table. Bar graphs will show for your ratings, community ratings, ABV, and IBU. Pie charts will display your total number of beers by year, month, and day of the week. A map will display the location of the the breweries that the beers come from. A table will display the info for each beer, with columns for brewey info, beer info with description, style info, your rating, community ratinng, ABV, and IBU.

Wishlist

The wishlist page will display the beers that have been added to the users Untappd wishlist. If the user has no beers in their wishlist a message will indicate the fact that the wish list is empty
